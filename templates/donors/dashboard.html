Welcome to the Chernobyl Tissue Bank Biosample Search
The Chernobyl Tissue Bank Biosample Search is a feature that provides information about the availability of biological samples for research purposes.

The biosample search can be refined using a number of clinical parameters (e.g. TNM status, AJCC Clinical stage and treatment and outcome). Once an investigator has identified the biosamples they wish to access, they may submit an application to obtain samples or information. To learn more about the application process, please visit our Research page. Applications must be approved before any samples can be released.

If you have any queries, please email these to nciwebtools@gmail.com.
