Welcome to the Chernobyl Tissue Bank Biosample Search
The Chernobyl Tissue Bank Biosample Search allows you to check whether the number of biological samples with the associated information you require for your research are available within the CTB.

After completing your biosample search you can further refine your search using a number of clinical parameters (e.g. TNM status, AJCC Clinical stage and treatment and outcome). If you do not wish to refine your search, you can skip this step. Once you have defined the biosamples that you wish to access (with or without additional clinical information), you will have the option to download an application form. Instructions on submission of applications for access can be found on the form. Information about the review process of applications is available on the Access to Materials page. Applications must be approved before any samples can be released.

In your application, is important that you specify the criteria for sample selection as exactly as possible, by completing all sections of the Filter. If your application is approved, samples will be provided according to the search criteria you have used in the Filter. Screen shots of the biosample search may be appended to your application if you wish.

The search facility will provide you with the number of cases that fit your search criteria – you will not receive detailed information on the individual cases until your project is approved.

Samples issued following approval of the project will be accompanied by the following dataset – CTB unique patient identifier, sample unique identifier, pathological diagnosis, age at the accident, age at operation, gender, and oblast (administrative region) of residence at the time of the accident. Information on estimated thyroid radiation dose can also be provided if justified in your application. Clinical information as requested using the clinical search facility can also be provided on each case, providing it has been justified in the application.

If you have any queries, please email these to ctb-info@isb-cgc.org. Online information using the search tools is available where you see the icon.
