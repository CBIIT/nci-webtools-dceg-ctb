WELCOME TO
Chernobyl Tissue Bank
The CTB is a research tissue bank that was established to collect, document, store, and issue to bona fide researchers, biological samples donated by patients with thyroid tumours who live in the areas of Ukraine and Russia close to the site of the Chernobyl Nuclear Power Plant.
If you are looking for information on the Chernobyl accident itself please go to Podcasts & Videos and General Information about the Chernobyl Accident.
