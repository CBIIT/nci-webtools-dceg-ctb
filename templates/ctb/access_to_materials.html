Applications to access biomaterials must be submitted via the Biosample Search facility. All applications for use of the bank are referred to an independent External Review Panel, which includes experts nominated by NCI and the Institute in Ukraine.

Distribution of materials to a Principal Investigator whose project has been approved will be authorized subject to:
  1. receipt of a signed agreement from the Principal Investigator to conform to the conditions set out in the Material and Data Transfer Agreement;
  2. modification of the number and size of samples required dependent on any amendments by the External Review Panel and the availability of the material.
Each sample will be identified by a CTB sample ID and will be accompanied by a minimum data set comprising the following information:
  • age at accident - complying with HIPAA regulations
  • age at operation - complying with HIPAA regulations
  • sex
  • oblast of residence
  • Diagnosis
  • Estimated thyroid radiation dose (if requested by the researcher).

If a project requires more clinical information than the minimum data set, this must be stated clearly in the application, particularly if this is an essential component of the project. Any such project will normally be carried out as a collaboration with the NCI and the IEM and, where appropriate, approval of the relevant Ethical Committee must be sought.

Where possible, material for a given project will be provided in one batch, however, in some cases, provision may have to be made over a period of time.
